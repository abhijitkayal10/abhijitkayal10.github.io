## â“ What is the Bechdel Test?

A movie **passes the Bechdel Test** if:

1. It has **at least two named female characters**
2. They **talk to each other**
3. Their conversation is **not about a man**

While simple, the test is a powerful indicator of **gender bias and representation** in cinema.
